Uniform Services

Uniform Design Services in Saudi Arabia

At MAQ MHA Trading Company, we offer a comprehensive uniform services solution in Saudi Arabia. We handle every aspect, from uniform design to manufacturing, to meet our customers' needs for their company's objectives and branding requirements. Our range of customized uniform options includes embroidered uniforms, various fabrics, patterns, and logo printing services such as heat transfer and silkscreen.

Our dedicated customer service team works closely with each customer to create growth opportunities, address concerns, and develop tailored solutions. We operate multiple professional stitching units staffed with experienced personnel who excel in designing and tailoring uniforms. All projects undergo thorough research to meet the specific requirements of your institution and deliver garments that combine quality, style, comfort, and functionality

Our in-house design team strives to create exclusive designs and colors that enhance the image of your organization. Our flexible service and supply concept allows you to focus on your core business while efficiently outsourcing your uniform needs. We ensure that our uniform solutions align with your company's color scheme, brand guidelines, and key requirements.

By choosing MAQ MHA Trading Company for your uniform services, you can expect a seamless and cost-effective experience that delivers high-quality uniforms while upholding your company's brand identity.

Design Stitching Screen Printing Embroidery

Designing Stitching

We have multiple stitching units and a team of qualified design experts who work together to deliver uniforms that offer a seamless combination of quality, style, comfort, and exceptional durability. Additionally, our long-term partnerships with reliable and responsible fabric vendors guarantee our customers excellent quality control, timely deliveries, and competitive factory-direct pricing.

Screen Printing

At MAQ MHA Trading Company, we offer screen printing services on a wide range of items including uniforms, umbrellas, bags, and various materials. Depending on the specific requirements of the project, we utilize the latest digital technology as well as traditional techniques for screen printing. With our capabilities, we can manufacture custom T-shirts and other apparel featuring your full-color designs.

Screen printing at MAQ MHA Trading Co. provides several advantages over alternative options, such as cost-effectiveness, vibrant colors, and long-lasting prints. Custom-printed uniforms convey a sense of professionalism and reliability, showcasing the company as a trustworthy source. By investing in high-quality and appealing uniforms, companies can greatly enhance their overall image.

Embroidery

At MAQ MHA Trading Company, we offer world-class embroidery services for a wide range of garments, including uniforms, shirts, hats, and other clothing items. We recognize the critical impact of first impressions, whether it be during client meetings or through front desk representatives. To help businesses leave a lasting mark, we provide custom-made logo embroidery services that are not only cost-effective but also serve as a creative and impactful way to promote and advertise brands..

Our in-house embroidery facility ensures the highest level of quality as we meticulously inspect every garment. With the aid of our state-of-the-art embroidery machines and advanced software, we have the capacity to handle large-scale projects efficiently and deliver stunning, multicolored embroidery within shorter timeframes. This enables us to meet the demands of our clients while maintaining fashionable and up-to-date embroidery designs.

Our commitment to quality, efficiency, and creativity sets us apart as a trusted provider of world-class embroidery solutions.

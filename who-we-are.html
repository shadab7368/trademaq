WHO WE ARE

Uniform Manufacturer & Supplier in UAE

MAQ MHA Trading Company is one of the leading uniforms supplier in the UAE. We are a prominent uniform designer, Uniform manufacturer & supplier in Saudi Arabia. We are firmly rooted in providing services to a wide range of industries, including MNCs, local corporations, the food and beverage sector, government bodies, hospitals, hotels, and international schools, etc. We offer corporate apparel, accessories, school uniform, corporate gifts and much more.

We are committed to research, develop and supply the highest quality uniforms of modern style at competitive prices. We maintain high-quality standards according to the international parameters. All our uniforms supplies go through quality checks by our dedicated team before they are shipped to the customers.

The materials we use in the uniform fabrics are thoroughly tested and guaranteed to provide comfort and satisfaction. Besides the quality, we give importance to the size, fitting and color of the uniforms, as we know that they add to the image and personality of the wearer.

Our innovative and functional designs have also satisfied different clientele range such as Coca-Cola, Al Rawabi Dairy Company, Menasco Contracting, Flemingo International, IFFCO International, Aujan and other reputed companies.

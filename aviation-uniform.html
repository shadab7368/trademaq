AVIATION UNIFORM

We are a manufacturer and supplier of uniform clothing for the aviation industry. We have a large choice of pilot shirts, knitwear, full uniforms, and high-visibility safety clothing. Our substitute slim-fit pilot shirts are very popular with all aviation workers who need quality pilot shirts and are always available in stock.All our uniform packages come with personalization options. This allows airlines to match the uniform appearance to brand guidelines, colors, and corporate identity. As one of the leading airline uniform suppliers, we know that all airlines are different and require different solutions. We guide you through the entire process – from design drawings and prototyping to individual fittings and the final uniforms.

We provide pilots and all airport staff with everything. Our client list includes KSA airports, flight schools, business and commercial airlines. We welcome inquiries from major airlines to pilots. A complete uniform or pilot shirt may be required. Thank you for your business.

ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Uniform Manufacturer & Suppliers in Saudi Arabia

MAQ MHA Trading Company is one of the leading uniform suppliers in Saudi Arabia. We specialize in designing, manufacturing, and supplying high-quality uniforms. Our services cater to a wide range of industries, including MNCs, local corporations, the food and beverage sector, government bodies, hospitals, hotels, and international schools. In addition to uniforms, we offer corporate apparel, accessories, school uniforms, corporate gifts, and more.

At MAQ MHA Trading Company, we are dedicated to researching, developing, and providing the highest quality uniforms that feature modern styles, all at competitive prices. We uphold rigorous quality standards in accordance with international parameters. Before being shipped to customers, all our uniform supplies undergo thorough quality checks by our dedicated team.

We prioritize the comfort and satisfaction of our customers by using materials that are extensively tested and guaranteed to meet their expectations. Furthermore, we understand the significance of size, fitting, and color in enhancing the image and personality of the wearer.

The materials we use in the uniform fabrics are thoroughly tested and guaranteed to provide comfort and satisfaction. Besides the quality, we give importance to the size, fitting and color of the uniforms, as we know that they add to the image and personality of the wearer.

Our uniforms are designed with you in mind. We recognize that uniforms are a symbol of respect for a company's staff. They play a vital role in boosting the performance and confidence of employees, instilling in them a sense of pride, responsibility, and professionalism. Corporate clothing serves as a powerful visual representation of a company's brand identity, allowing consumers to establish an immediate connection. As a company's brand awareness grows, consumers will easily associate specific design elements, colors, symbols, and logos with that brand.

At MAQ MHA Trading Company, we collaborate closely with our clients to develop customized programs that align with their specific requirements and budget. A dedicated service representative ensures that you are always 100% satisfied with our service. We believe in providing you and your employees the opportunity to examine garment samples before making a purchase. This allows you to physically experience the fabric, study the detailing, and ensure that it meets your expectations. We value our client relationships and strive to design and deliver the ideal corporate uniform that reflects their unique image.

Overall, MAQ MHA Trading Company is committed to delivering exceptional service and ensuring your utmost satisfaction. We never expect you to make a purchase solely based on information from a catalog. Instead, we provide the means for you to make an informed decision by evaluating garment samples firsthand. We prioritize your needs and preferences throughout the process of designing and acquiring your corporate uniforms.
